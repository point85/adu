<h3>San Miguel</h3>
<img src="../resources/images/San_Miguel_Perspective.png" alt="San Miguel" style="width:100%; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
<p>The San Miguel is a one bedroom 1 1/2 bathroom ADU with 705 sq. ft. of living area.  It is rendered in traditional style with a covered front porch.</p>
<ul>
<li>The kitchen has a microwave over the range with a full-size refrigerator and tall pantry cabinet. There is table seating for five people.</li>
<li>The San Miguel has a hall closet and a linen closet. The primary bath is a tub/shower combination.</li>
<li>Space is provided for a full-size stacked washer and dryer laundry pair.</li>
<li>Heating and air conditioning is provided by a ductless mini-split heat pump.</li>
<li>Hot water is provided by an electric storage tank unit. A gas or heat pump storage tank will require an external access door with enclosed walls (not shown).</li>
</ul>
